What is the SUPERgroup?

The SUPERgroup (Security, Usability, & Privacy Education & Research group) is an interdisciplinary research collective comprising over 20 members at the University of Chicago. We focus on the intersection between computer security, privacy, and usability, as evidenced by our recent publications. Our current projects focus on using data-driven methods to improve online security and privacy, as well as improving interaction aspects of both the Internet of Things and interactive machine learning systems.

The SUPERgroup is led by Blase Ur, a Neubauer Family Assistant Professor at the University of Chicago.

This extension was created by the SUPERgroup Tracking Transparency team of Euirim Choi, Claire Dolin, Aaron Goldman, Chang Min Hahn, Sixiong Shan, and Ben Weinshel.
