About the Extension

The Tracking Transparency extension was built at the University of Chicago by Euirim Choi, Claire Dolin, Aaron Goldman, Chang Min Hahn, Sixiong Shan, and Ben Weinshel.

We are led by Blase Ur, an assistant professor at the University of Chicago. We also work alongside our external collaborators, Michelle Mazurek, an assistant professor at the University of Maryland and Lorrie Cranor, a professor at Carnegie Mellon University.
